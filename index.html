SCHOOL ACHIEVMENTS

Jr.Diplomathon
Sandipani’s enthusiastic team of 44 students (class VII and VIII) actively participated in the Junior Diplomathon Conference, organised by Jain International School dated February 24 and 25, 2018. Sandipanians have proved their excellence and won prizes in all the committees. Two students have bagged the tag of jr Diplomats as the first prize. One student won second prize as the mountain climber and 12 students received third prize for better performance.
Scipotech-Science and Technology competition
Sandipani students participated enthusiastically in the various categories of Scipotech-Science and Technology competition organised by Poddar International School, Koradi on November 27, 2017. In the Junior Category, Achyut Gharpure and Aaratrika Chakraborty bagged second prize in the display of non-working scientific models. In the senior category, Vedant Ijhamulwar and Ameya Arora won first prize in the display of working scientific models. Where as in Technological part of Scipotech Mihir Sangode and Shaily Bangre of class IX brought the laurels to the school by bagging second Prize in ‘Best out of Waste Competition. In STEM (Robotics) Pranav Nayyar and Shubhankar Bhattacharya of class VIII stole the show and bagged first prize. In the animation on saving environment competition Sarthak Barai and Shardul Bawankar received third prize for their presentation.
Debate competition 2017-2018
The topic for debate was in the opinion of the house – ‘We need to disconnect to connect.’ Students from 21 schools of Nagpur have participated in the same, where Swastika Kar, a student of class VIII C of Sandipani won the appreciation and was declared as upcoming debater of the year. She was awarded with a trophy, certificate and a cash prize of ` 300. Other participant were Shweta Jhambia ( class IX B) who received a participation certificate.
